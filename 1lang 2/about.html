Rooted in Purity

From our Portland apothecary, we believe clean, potent botanicals are the key to healthy skin. Our mission is to create effective, transparent, and eco-conscious skincare for a naturally radiant you.

Our Story

Noctis was founded in 2023 by Elena Reed on a simple idea: skincare should be pure. Based in the heart of Portland, Oregon, we are inspired by the nature that surrounds us, and we hold ourselves to a higher standard of clean.

We were tired of harsh chemicals, hidden toxins, and confusing ingredient lists. Our commitment is to create 'clean-clinical' formulas that use the best of nature, perfected by science. Our bright, clean aesthetic represents our promise of purity and transparency. Our name and logo represent our ingredients: powerful, natural, and earthy. We harness the potency of plant-actives like Bakuchiol and blend them with safe, proven hydrators like Hyaluronic Acid. This is skincare you can trust.
